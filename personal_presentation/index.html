Research Overview: interpretability in Deep Learning for Astrophysics

Goal: Develop a Real/Bogus classifier with no human labelling for a Vera Rubin Observatory-scale dataset, with a focus on interpretability and uncertainty quantification.

Methods: Weakly Supervised Learning, Uncertainty Quantification, Latent Space Exploration.

Research Overview: interpretability in Deep Learning for Theoretical Physics

Goal: Adapt and develop Shapley Values for probing the Parton Distribution Function theoretical framework.

Methods: Explainable Artificial Intelligence, Uncertainty Quantification, Inverse Problem.

Future research: interpretability in Deep Learning for Neutrino Physics

Goal: Developed Mechanistic Interpretability methods for Foundation models for Neutrino Physics, probing the model understanding of neutrino interactions.

Methods: Mechanistic Interpretability, Foundation Models, Sparse Latent representations.
